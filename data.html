Skills

Essential skills needed for excellent performance in IT field divided mainly as two types, IT specific skills and generic skills. According to the data from burning glass IT specific skills in our required skill set rank in descending order in the demand from employers as follows SQL, JavaScript, Java, technical support, C#, Linux, .Net programming, HTML, python, SQL server and technical industry knowledge. Descending order of generic skills is Communication skills, problem solving, organizational skills, writing, teamwork, creativity, time management, presentation skills, meeting deadlines and analytical skills.

After the clear analysis of burning glass facts provided we found out some highest ranked skills in demand are not in our required skill set. Among those the first three IT specific skills are building relationships, Business management and ITIL. Detail orientation, quality assurance and control and mentoring are the general skills in market that we are not included in our skill set.
